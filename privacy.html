Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

PulseSave is built to help users manage their finances with clarity and confidence. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Effective Date: March 2026
Quick summary: We collect the information you provide, use it to operate and improve PulseSave, do not sell your data, and use secure services such as Firebase to store app information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use PulseSave:

  • Email address used for account login and authentication
  • Financial information you manually enter, such as income, expenses, budgets, savings, and goals
  • Basic usage information that helps us improve app performance and reliability

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide budgeting tools, calculations, and financial insights
  • Personalize your experience inside PulseSave
  • Improve app features, stability, and overall user experience
  • Help maintain app security and account integrity

3. AI-Generated Insights

PulseSave may use AI to generate budgeting suggestions and financial insights. These outputs are provided for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial, investment, tax, or legal advice.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data submitted through PulseSave may be stored using trusted cloud infrastructure such as Firebase. We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to help protect your information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that support app functionality, hosting, authentication, analytics, or subscription infrastructure, only as needed to operate the service.

6. Subscriptions & Payments

PulseSave offers a 14-day free trial. After the trial, a subscription fee of $2.99 per month may be charged through Apple. Payment processing is handled by Apple, and PulseSave does not store your full payment details.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and related data. You may also stop using the app at any time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of PulseSave after updates become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
