Account Deletion

Delete your account.

You can permanently delete your PulseSave account and all associated data directly from the app at any time.

Effective Date: March 2026
⚠️
This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, all your data is immediately and irreversibly removed from our systems.

📱 How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open PulseSave on your device.
  2. Tap the ☰ menu icon in the top right corner of the Home screen.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your identity and tap Delete to permanently remove your account.

🗑️ What Gets Deleted

Once confirmed, the following data is permanently and immediately deleted:

  • Account information and login credentials
  • Email address and profile information
  • Bills and recurring expenses
  • Income records
  • Expense and transaction history
  • Savings goals and debt plans
  • AI Coach chat history
  • Subscription-related app data

After deletion, your data cannot be recovered. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use PulseSave again.
