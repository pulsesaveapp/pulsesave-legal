Delete Your PulseSave Account

PulseSave allows users to permanently delete their account directly from the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open PulseSave.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Once you confirm account deletion, PulseSave permanently removes your account and all associated data from our database.

The following data is permanently deleted:

  • Account information
  • Email address and profile information
  • Bills and recurring expenses
  • Income records
  • Expense history
  • Savings goals
  • AI Coach chat history
  • Subscription-related app data

After deletion, your data cannot be recovered and you will need to create a new account if you wish to use PulseSave again.
